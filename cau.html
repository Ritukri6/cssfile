Accommodation

A clean, safe, and spacious place to stay is what we provide! Many children come from deprived backgrounds and need extreme care for their proper development, all of which starts with ensuring their safety and hygiene. These children are offered a home in the Narayan Seva Sansthan campus in the dormitories assigned to them.

Food & Nutrition

Good Food, Good Life! Proper nutrition is crucial for growing children. They need a balanced diet for appropriate growth, and for this, we have a team of dieticians who monitor their diet and ensure they are provided three full meals each day, along with milk and healthy snacks, twice a day. This routine is followed religiously, to ensure no child goes to bed hungry.

Healthcare

The health of these children is our topmost priority and the availability of proper medical and healthcare assistance is a big part of it. There are regular medical check-ups of the children at our facility, to ensure that there are no underlying health issues that we may have missed. In case a child is diagnosed with a health issue, immediate medical assistance is offered to them.

Co-Curricular Activities

All round development of the child requires emotional, physical, and moral development which is complemented by co-curricular activities. Thus, apart from free education, we conduct activities like arts and crafts, painting, skill development classes and sports for the differently-abled like cricket, football, basketball etc.
